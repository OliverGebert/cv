Delivery Management

Throughout my career, I have consistently delivered complex, high-impact IT initiatives that drive strategic value and cross-functional alignment. One of my key successes was leading the rollout of an enterprise-wide API management platform, where I established an API-first strategy and a Center of Expertise that significantly improved system interoperability and integration capabilities across business units. Before that, I also led the evaluation and corporate-wide implementation of a ServiceNow-based Enterprise Architecture (EA) solution, enhancing transparency, governance, and application portfolio management across a multi divisional organization. My delivery leadership was also instrumental in the global rollout of SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting, where I aligned stakeholders and solution partners such as SAP and PwC to ensure seamless implementation. From integration strategy to architectural governance, I bring a strong focus on execution excellence and lasting business impact.
