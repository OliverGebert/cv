Digital Transformation

As a senior IT leader, I have driven several key digital transformation initiatives across the Enabling Functions. My engagement began with the global Transforming HR program, where I played a central role in consolidating fragmented national SAP HCM systems into a unified global ERP solution. One of my key responsibilities was leading the SAP HCM rollout in Spain, ensuring alignment with the global template while managing country-specific requirements and stakeholder expectations. Building on this foundation, I later led the technical team of SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting, working closely with global partners like SAP and PwC. This initiative marked a strategic move to modernize and cloud-enable the company’s talent acquisition processes, achieving improved efficiency, usability, and global standardization. Most recently, I spearheaded the introduction of an API management platform for Enabling Functions, based on Mulesoft. This initiative established an API-first strategy, significantly enhanced system interoperability, and laid the groundwork for a more agile and scalable integration architecture across business platforms.
