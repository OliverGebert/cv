Global HRIT Operations

As Head of Global Operations for SAP HCM Enabling Services, I was accountable for delivering stable, high-quality global HR IT operations across key domains such as Personnel Administration (PA), Organizational Management (OM), Master Data, Authorization, and User Management. A major milestone during my tenure was the successful service transition from Bayer Germany to Bayer India. This complex initiative involved setting up an ITIL-based incident and problem management framework, designing a comprehensive training and handover plan, and leading cross-cultural change management to ensure knowledge transfer and operational continuity. I oversaw the onboarding and upskilling of the new team, fostering their capability to independently manage core HR IT functions. The transition resulted in measurable improvements in operational KPIs, including reduced ticket re-openings and faster resolution times, while maintaining service quality during a time of significant organizational change.
