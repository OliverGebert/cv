Integration Architecture

In my role leading platform integration and excellence initiatives, we developed and executed a forward-looking integration strategy for the global HR IT landscape. Historically reliant on ERP-internal ALE connections and FTP-based batch file exchanges, the architecture was fragmented with redundant interfaces, latency issues, and challenges in ensuring consistent data quality—key for GDPR compliance. Our strategic shift embraced an API-first and streaming-based middleware approach, significantly reducing interface redundancy and improving data consistency and system responsiveness. This transformation not only enhanced current operational reliability but also established a scalable, flexible foundation that supports future migrations and integration demands across platforms. This evolution was driven by a team of certified IT architects, empowered through a dedicated Center of Expertise and continuous professional development aligned with cutting-edge integration patterns and architectural best practices.
